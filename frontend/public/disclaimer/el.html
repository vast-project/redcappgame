Declaration of Informed Consent to Participate in Research Activity

Research Project: Values Across Space & Time (VAST), https://www.vast-project.eu/
Funded by EU Horizon 2020 research programme (Grant Agreement n°101004949).

Research Activity:Exploration of European Values in Classical Texts and Folklore.

Organiser: Stefano Montanelli, Department of Computer Science, Università degli Studi di Milano, stefano.montanelli@unimi.it
For any query and further information, please contact vast@islab.di.unimi.it.

Things I need to know:
  1. Purpose of the research activity: To study the moral elements that can be considered as the “common” and “fundamental” values of the European Union; to investigate how the European Values are recognized and perceived in classical texts and folklore (e.g., fairy tales); to understand how the today citizens appropriate of these values; to store and share aggregated data about the appropriation of values by the citizens.
  2. Description of Action: we propose to explore an interactive version of the “Little Red Riding Hood” fairy tale. Throughout the story, we propose questions where you can express your feelings about a specific step of the story by choosing between two proposed options. The answer to a question can change the subsequent steps of the story. At the end of the activity, you will be asked to evaluate the experience on a 1-5 scale.
  3. Voluntary Participation: I understand that my participation is voluntary. I do not have to take part if I do not want to. I may leave the group at any time for any reason.
  4. Risks: By participating in the survey, I may reveal perceptions, beliefs, opinions, or preferences. However, these will not be linked to my identity.
  5. Third parties: The VAST project may share the data generated by me exclusively in an anonymous and aggregated form via the VAST web platform. VAST will ensure that this data is not linkable to my identity in any way.
  6. Confidentiality / Privacy: By registering on this website, I’m asked to provide a) age; b) gender; c) level of education. The Research Activity Organiser collects, stores, and processes the data to the extent necessary and the sole purpose to evaluate the research activity findings and to anonymously communicate the results of such evaluation.
  7. Retention Period: The collected data will be stored for one month on a server at the Department of Computer Science, Università degli Studi di Milano. After one month, any data originally collected from me will be deleted. Only anonymous and aggregated data will be saved for subsequent project activities. Access to the collected data will be limited to the members of the research staff of the Università degli Studi di Milano involved in the VAST project.
